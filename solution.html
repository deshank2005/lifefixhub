Check if it's just your home
Look outside to see if neighbors have power. If their lights are off too, it's likely a neighborhood outage.
Inspect your circuit breaker
Locate your electrical panel (usually in basement/garage). Open it and look for switches that are in the "OFF" position or between ON/OFF.
Reset tripped breakers
Flip fully OFF, then back ON. You should feel resistance and hear a click. Don't force it if it won't reset!
Check GFCI outlets
Press the "RESET" button on any outlets with test/reset buttons (common in kitchens/bathrooms).
Contact your utility company
Call their outage line or check their website/app for outage reports in your area.
