🚗 Fixing a Car That Won't Start

⚡ Emergency Edition! ⚡

🔋 🔧 ⚠️

🛠️ Tools/Items Needed:

🔋 Jumper Cables 🧤 Insulated Gloves 🔦 Flashlight 📱 Roadside Assistance

🚨 Emergency Situation If:

You smell gasoline 💨, see smoke 💨, or hear loud knocking sounds 🔨

🔍 Diagnosis Steps:

1 Listen to what happens when you turn the key 🔑
  • Clicking sound: Weak battery 🔋 (most common)
  • Single loud click: Possible starter motor issue ⚙️
  • Engine cranks but won't start: Fuel ⛽ or spark 🔥 problem
  • Complete silence: Battery connection 🔌 or security system 🛡️

🔄 Jump-Start Procedure:

2 Position donor car correctly 🚗💨🚗

Park nose-to-nose or side-by-side. ⚠️ Never touch cables to moving parts! Turn off both engines.

3 Connect jumper cables in EXACT order:
  1. 🔴 Red to dead (+) terminal
  2. 🔴 Red to donor (+) terminal
  3. ⚫ Black to donor (-) terminal
  4. ⚫ Black to bare metal on dead car (engine block recommended)
⚠️ DANGER! Never connect black to dead (-) terminal first - risk of explosion! 💥
