🚩 When to Seek Help:

If stress causes chest pain, difficulty breathing, or thoughts of self-harm, contact a professional immediately.

🌬️ 4-7-8 Breathing Technique

😌
Sit comfortably with straight spine
Place tongue behind front teeth
Inhale quietly through nose for 4 seconds
Hold breath for 7 seconds
Exhale completely through mouth for 8 seconds
Repeat 4 cycles (calms nervous system in 1 minute)

🌳 5-4-3-2-1 Grounding Method

5 things you can see (describe textures/details)
4 things you can touch (notice temperatures/textures)
3 things you can hear (distant sounds too)
2 things you can smell (or pleasant smell memories)
1 thing you can taste (sip water or chew gum)

💆 Progressive Muscle Relaxation

Close eyes, take 3 deep breaths
Tense forehead for 5 seconds → release
Squeeze eyes shut → release
Clench jaw → release
Continue downward (shoulders, arms, etc.)
