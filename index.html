⚡ Power Cut at Home?

Solution: Check circuit breakers, use UPS/inverter, report to electricity board.

🚗 Car Won't Start?

Solution: Check battery terminals, jump-start, or call mechanic.

😥 Feeling Stressed?

Solution: Take deep breaths, meditate, talk to a friend.

🚰 Pipe Under Sink Leaking?

Solution: Turn off water valve, apply epoxy putty, or replace washer.

📱 Phone Getting Too Hot?

Solution: Close background apps, remove case, avoid direct sunlight.

📶 Wi-Fi Keeps Dropping?

Solution: Restart router, check for interference, update firmware.

🧘 Chronic Lower Back Pain?

Solution: Do cat-cow stretches, improve posture, use lumbar support.

💳 Unrecognized Credit Card Charge?

Solution: Freeze card immediately, call bank, dispute transaction.

🚿 Shower Drain Clogged?

Solution: Use a plunger, pour baking soda + vinegar, or use a drain snake.

🔐 Forgot Safe Combination?

Solution: Try default codes, contact manufacturer, or use a locksmith.

📲 Can’t Install an App?

Solution: Check storage, update your OS, clear Play Store cache, or check internet connection.
